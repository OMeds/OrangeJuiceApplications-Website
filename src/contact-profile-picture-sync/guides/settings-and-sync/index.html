Photo Sources

Turn platforms on or off for discovery — Gravatar, GitHub, LinkedIn, Google, etc.

Disabled sources are skipped during Find Photo and Sync Social Photos.

Sync & Automation

  • Auto-apply thresholds — control when high-confidence matches skip manual review
  • Background sync — optional scheduled refresh (subject to iOS background limits)
  • Duplicate merging — optionally combine duplicate contact cards with the same name, phone, or email

Contacts tab filters

Before syncing, filter the list:

  • Favorites — starred contacts only
  • Missing photos — contacts without a profile image
  • Pending review — contacts with open suggestions

Use sort options to prioritize who gets matched first.

Favorites & shortcuts

  • Star contacts in the list to mark favorites.
  • Use the Sync Favorites Siri shortcut or home-screen widget to refresh favorite contacts quickly.

Privacy controls

Under Settings → Privacy & Security:

  • Privacy Dashboard — export data, disconnect providers, wipe app data
  • Security Settings — biometric lock, URL policy
  • Photo Audit Log — local history of applied photos
  • Photo Discovery Diagnostics — test matching pipelines
