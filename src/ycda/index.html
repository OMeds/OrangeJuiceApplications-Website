Inclusive weekly classes

Tap a class to explore age ranges, timing, and what makes each session special. Book tasters on the live site.

Spark

Recommended 3–7 years 4:30 pm – 5:15 pm

Inclusive dance and play — creative movement for early years and families. All welcome; contact Abi and the team if you are outside this age range.

Ignite

Recommended 5–11 years 5:15 pm – 6:00 pm

Inclusive dance with games and choreography — ideal for school-age dancers. Teens and adults in this style are welcome — speak to the team first.

Shine

Recommended 12+ years 6:00 pm – 6:45 pm

Inclusive dance for teens and adults — stronger choreography and performance skills in a supportive group.

Broadway Workout

Adults 17–100 6:15 pm – 7:00 pm Taster £5 · £20/month

Perfect for former dancers and musical theatre enthusiasts — dance to big show tunes, get fit, and meet like-minded people.

GLOW

Ages 5–11 (SEND) 5:15 pm – 6:00 pm Taster £8 · £35/month

Inclusive dance for children with special educational needs and disabilities who can engage in small groups and follow spoken instructions with teacher support.
