**You Can Dance Academy** runs inclusive classes at Blaby & District Social Centre. Orange Juice Applications builds and maintains the digital experience at [youcandanceacademy.co.uk](https://youcandanceacademy.co.uk).

## What families get

- Weekly styles: Spark, Ignite, Shine, Broadway Workout, and GLOW.
- Taster bookings and term information without phone tag.
- Member portal integration with ClassManager for enrolled families.

## Policies for members

Member-facing privacy and terms live on the **studio site**, not the FaceMatch legal pack. From our [YCDA hub](/ycda/), use the studio links for class policies; FaceMatch policies apply only to the contact photo app.
