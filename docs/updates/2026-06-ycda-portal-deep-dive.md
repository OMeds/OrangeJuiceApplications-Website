[You Can Dance Academy](https://youcandanceacademy.co.uk) is our anchor studio partner. Here is what we run in production today.

## The problem

Families needed one place to understand inclusive classes (Spark, Ignite, Shine, Broadway Workout, GLOW), book tasters without phone tag, and manage member tasks — while staff avoided duplicate admin across ClassManager and email.

## What we built

- **Public studio site** — class storytelling, FAQs, team, and SEND-friendly messaging
- **Taster and enrolment flows** — clear pricing and policies; hand-off to ClassManager where bookings live
- **Member portal** — schedules, billing context, practice videos, and family tools
- **Parent app links** — mobile presence when the native apps publish

## Outcome

Less admin for Abi and the team; families spend more time on dance, less on chasing links. The same patterns we use for YCDA — portals, integrations, mobile — are what we offer other studios and membership businesses.
