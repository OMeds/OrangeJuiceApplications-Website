FaceMatch is still in development, but families and reviewers should not wait for an App Store badge to understand how the app works.

We published the [FaceMatch preview](/facematch/), a full [guides hub](/contact-profile-picture-sync/guides/) with ten step-by-step articles, and the privacy, security, and subscription pages that will ship with the app. The homepage now includes an interactive preview — the same pattern we use to show the live YCDA studio site.

**Why it matters:** On-device contact photo matching is sensitive. Publishing support and policy pages early is how we build trust — for our own products and for client portals where parents log in and share data.

**Next:** App Store listing when review completes. [Join the beta](/facematch/beta/) if you want early access on iPhone or iPad.
