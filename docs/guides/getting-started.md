# Getting started with FaceMatch

FaceMatch updates profile photos in your **Apple Contacts** address book. Everything runs on your iPhone or iPad — you choose what to apply.

---

## What you need

- An iPhone or iPad with the FaceMatch app installed from the App Store
- **Contacts permission** when the app asks (required for core features)
- Optional: email, phone, or social URLs on contact cards for better matching

---

## First launch

1. Open **FaceMatch** and complete the welcome screens.
2. Tap **Allow** when iOS asks for **Contacts** access.
3. Optional: connect social accounts during onboarding, or skip and sign in later.
4. Tap **Continue** to open your contact list.

---

## Ways to get photos from social networks

FaceMatch supports **three options** for Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google, and other networks:

| Option | Where to go | When to use |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Account Sign-In** | **Settings → Account Sign-In** | Connect an account for automated API matching |
| **Import from Files** | **Settings → Import from Files** | Import exports (LinkedIn CSV, Google Takeout, Meta JSON) or photo folders |
| **Share from Safari** | Contact → **Share Photo from Browser** | Save one profile photo from the browser |

Use whichever fits — none are required. FaceMatch always matches from data on contact cards.

Full walkthrough: [Ways to get photos](/contact-profile-picture-sync/guides/two-paths-social-networks/)

---

## Three ways to update photos

| Goal | Where to go |
|------|-------------|
| Match **one person** | **Contacts** → open a contact → **Find Photo for This Contact** |
| Match **many people** | **Contacts** → **Sync Social Photos** |
| Check suggestions first | **Review** tab → approve or reject matches |
| **Import an export** | **Settings** → **Import from Files** → choose file → **Review** tab |
| **Save from Safari** | Contact → **Share Photo from Browser** → Share → FaceMatch (or library fallback) |

---

## Recommended first run

1. Open a contact → **Edit Contact** → add a LinkedIn or Facebook profile URL if you have one.
2. Run **Find Photo for This Contact**.
3. Open the **Review** tab if a suggestion appears — tap to approve or dismiss.
4. Confirm the photo updated in Apple Contacts.

Optional next steps:

- **Account Sign-In:** **Settings → Account Sign-In** → connect Google or LinkedIn → **Sync Social Photos**
- **Import from Files:** **Settings → Import from Files** → import LinkedIn Connections.csv or a photo folder
