# Optional social sign-in

Signing in is **not required**. FaceMatch already matches from names, emails, phones, and URLs on contact cards.

Use **Account Sign-In** when you want automated matching from official APIs after connecting an account. For exports and manual photos without sign-in, see [Import from files](/contact-profile-picture-sync/guides/import-from-files/) or [Ways to get photos](/contact-profile-picture-sync/guides/two-paths-social-networks/).

---

## Navigate to Account Sign-In

1. Open the **Settings** tab.
2. Tap **Account Sign-In** (under **Accounts & Activity**).
3. Choose a platform (LinkedIn, Google, Facebook, Instagram, Microsoft, Slack, X, Discord).
4. Tap **Sign In** and complete login in the browser sheet.
5. Return to FaceMatch — the platform should show **Connected**.

Alternative entry points:

- **Settings → Help & Legal → How to Get Photos** — platform requirements
- **Settings → Account Sign-In → Import from Files** — import an export without signing in

Disconnect anytime with **Disconnect** on the Account Sign-In screen.

---

## After sign-in

1. **Settings** → enable the platform under **Sync Preferences**.
2. **Contacts** → **Sync Social Photos** (bulk) or open one contact → **Find Photo for This Contact**.
3. **Review** tab → approve suggestions before they update Apple Contacts.

---

## Per platform

### LinkedIn

- **Sign-in:** vanity URLs and profile lookups when a LinkedIn URL is on the contact card.
- **Import:** **Connections.csv** under Settings → Import from Files.

### Google

- **Sign-in:** match iOS contacts to Google Contacts when emails align.
- **Import:** Google Takeout vCard/CSV without signing in.

### Facebook

- **Sign-in:** Graph API for profile links on contacts plus app friends who use FaceMatch.
- **Import:** Meta friends JSON or save photos from Safari.

### Instagram

- **Sign-in:** Business Discovery for @handles (Business or Creator account required).
- **Import:** followers/following JSON to add @handles to contacts.

### Microsoft / Slack

- **Sign-in:** workplace directory when colleague emails match.
- **Import:** photo folder; workplace photos still need sign-in.

### X / Discord

- **Sign-in:** @handles on cards when signed in.
- **Import:** photo folder or Safari save.

---

## Platform requirements

Open **Settings → How to Get Photos** in the app for details on:

- Instagram Business/Creator requirement
- Meta app tester mode for Facebook
- LinkedIn developer app approval
- Google People API scopes
