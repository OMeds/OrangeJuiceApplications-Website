# Services

Orange Juice Applications is an all-in-one UK software studio. One team from first conversation through launch and support — not a handoff chain.

## Web apps & member portals

Custom websites, booking flows, and logged-in areas for members, parents, or customers.

- Public marketing sites with clear calls to action
- Member / parent portals (schedules, billing, videos, account tools)
- Admin dashboards for your team
- ClassManager, booking, and payment integrations

**Example:** [You Can Dance Academy](/ycda/) — live studio site and portal at [youcandanceacademy.co.uk](https://youcandanceacademy.co.uk).

## Mobile apps

iPhone, iPad, and Android when reach matters — including parent apps linked from your main site.

- Native SwiftUI / Apple platforms where quality matters
- Cross-platform or Android when your audience needs it
- App Store submission support and ongoing releases

## Integrations & APIs

Connect the tools you already use so staff stop copying data between systems.

- Booking and membership platforms
- Payments and invoicing
- CRM, email, and internal webhooks
- Documented APIs for future partners

## Rescue & accelerate existing code

You own the repo — we join as a focused delivery partner.

- Audit and roadmap an existing GitHub (or other) codebase
- Add features, fix performance, or prepare for App Store / production
- Design, mobile expertise, or extra capacity for your in-house team

- TestFlight and App Store paths when you are ready
