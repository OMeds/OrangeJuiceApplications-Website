## You Can Dance Academy — Live

**Role:** Product & engineering partner  
**Stack:** Web member experience, ClassManager integration, parent-facing flows, portal login  
**Status:** Live at [youcandanceacademy.co.uk](https://youcandanceacademy.co.uk)

### Problem

Inclusive dance classes in Blaby needed a modern public presence, taster booking that families could complete online, and a member portal so parents were not juggling PDFs, email threads, and separate logins.

### Solution

We designed and maintain the full studio stack: marketing site, class pages, FAQ, taster/enrolment paths, ClassManager integration points, and a member portal for schedules and family tools. Content reflects real timetables and SEND-friendly class descriptions — not generic templates.

### Outcome

Families book and manage classes with less friction; staff publish updates in one place. This is our reference implementation for **studios and membership businesses**.

**Explore:** [YCDA hub](/ycda/) · [Studio site](https://youcandanceacademy.co.uk)

## FaceMatch — In development

**Role:** Product owner & developer  
**Stack:** SwiftUI, on-device Vision matching, Apple Contacts, OAuth bridges  
**Status:** App Store listing pending

### Problem

People accumulate contact photos in messages and social apps, but updating Apple Contacts manually is tedious — and many matching tools upload address books to the cloud.

### Solution

FaceMatch matches photos on-device with Apple Vision, shows a **review queue** before any contact changes, and supports social sign-in or file import. Guides, security, and legal pages are published before App Store release.

### Outcome

A privacy-first utility we dogfood ourselves — the same engineering discipline (published support, honest scoping, Apple-native quality) we bring to client work.

**Explore:** [FaceMatch preview](/facematch/) · [TestFlight beta](/facematch/beta/) · [Guides](/contact-profile-picture-sync/guides/) · [Legal hub](/legal/)
