# Accessibility statement

Orange Juice Applications aims to make [orangejuiceapplications.com](https://www.orangejuiceapplications.com) usable for as many people as possible, including users who rely on assistive technology.

## Our commitment

We follow the [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/) at Level AA where practical for a marketing site and published documentation.

This includes:

- Semantic HTML and logical heading order
- Sufficient colour contrast on text and controls (ongoing audits)
- Keyboard access for navigation, forms, and interactive previews
- Visible focus indicators on interactive elements
- Text alternatives for meaningful images and icons marked decorative where appropriate
- Respect for `prefers-reduced-motion` (reduced animation and ambient effects)

## Known limitations

- Some third-party embeds (for example Calendly scheduling when enabled) are controlled by external providers and may not meet the same standard.
- Live screenshots in product previews are decorative references to external sites.
- PDF or document downloads from press / legal pages depend on the authoring tool used to create them.

We review new pages and major redesigns for accessibility as part of our build process.

## Feedback and contact

If you have trouble using any part of this site, or need content in a different format, contact us:

- **Email:** [support@orangejuiceapplications.com](mailto:support@orangejuiceapplications.com)
- **Subject line:** Website accessibility

We aim to respond within **two business days** and will work with you on a reasonable alternative or fix.

## Cookies and analytics

This marketing site uses **minimal cookies**. We may load privacy-friendly analytics (Plausible) when configured — it does not use cross-site tracking cookies. See our [website privacy policy](/company/privacy/) for details.
