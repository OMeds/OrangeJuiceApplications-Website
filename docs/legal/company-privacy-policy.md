# Website Privacy Policy

**Orange Juice Applications**  
**Effective date:** 3 June 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

This policy describes how Orange Juice Applications (“we”, “us”) handles personal information when you use **orangejuiceapplications.com** and related marketing pages. It does **not** replace the [FaceMatch Privacy Policy](/contact-profile-picture-sync/privacy/), which applies to the FaceMatch app.

## Who we are

Orange Juice Applications builds software including You Can Dance Academy’s member experience and FaceMatch. For website privacy questions: **privacy@orangejuiceapplications.com**.

## What we collect

### Information you send us

When you use **Start a project**, **Contact**, or **Feedback** forms, we receive the fields you submit (name, email, project details, etc.). Submissions may be processed by our form provider (Formspree) and delivered to our email inbox.

### Analytics

We use [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io/) on this website. Plausible does not use cookies and does not collect personal data for advertising. Aggregated page views help us understand which content is useful. See Plausible’s documentation for their data practices.

### Server logs

Our hosting provider may log IP addresses, user agents, and request paths for security and reliability. We do not use these logs for marketing profiling.

## Legal bases (UK / EEA visitors)

We process inquiry data under **legitimate interests** (responding to business enquiries) and, where applicable, **contract** preparation. You may object to processing or request access, correction, or deletion by emailing **privacy@orangejuiceapplications.com**.

## How long we keep data

Project inquiries and support emails are kept as long as needed to respond and for reasonable business records (typically up to 24 months unless a longer period is required by law). Analytics aggregates are retained per our analytics provider’s settings.

## Children

This website is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child submitted personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

## International transfers

If you contact us from outside the UK, your message may be stored on systems in the UK or EEA-aligned providers we use.
